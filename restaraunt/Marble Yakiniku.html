Marble Yakiniku

A remarkably refined experience that perfectly complements the Glen Waverley entertainment district

Marble Yakiniku was a delight to eat at, I went there to celebrate my Bachelor’s graduation and the thought that ran through my mind was “If this is the reward of 5 years of study, I should get more degrees!”. On a serious note, the food was great, we ordered the A5 Japanese Wagyu & M9 Australian Wagyu Platter and teriyaki chicken.
The beef was incredible and worth the cost, easily in the top 1% of beef I have ever eaten, and the chicken had a sophistication and depth to it that I struggle to find at other restaraunts. Overall, it was a great meal, and definitely a worthy celebration for any milestones in life!

Rating: 9/10

Price: $$$

Suburb: Glen Waverley

Tags: Yakiniku, BBQ, Japnese
